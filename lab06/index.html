Coolmath Games Evaluation

1. What is the URL of the website?

https://www.coolmathgames.com

2. What is the name of the website?

Coolmath Games

3. Who is the site's target audience?

This website has creative and learning games targeting young kids, but can also be entertaining and useful for all ages.

4. How is the site organized?

I think this site is a mix of heiarchial and webbed because it has categories split into sub-categories but you also have the ability to choose another game whilst in a game.

5. Which CRAP Design Principle does it use?

Contrast: it uses the dark background of page to contrast with the covers of the games which are mostly bright. Repetition is used throughout the whole site and most pages use the same layout. Proximity is used with the groupings of categories.

6. What is the Audit Score according to the Accessibility Checker?

80.

7. What is the site's effectiveness?

I would say its very effective becaue it is very easy to play the games and the games do provide knowledge especially towards it's targeted audience.

8. What is the site's efficiency?

It's efficiency is the ability to click and be right into the next game.

9. How is the engagement?

It is very engaging because of the games but other than that there isn't much engagment.

10. Recommendation for Improvement

Add ability to save games and add a "Favorites" tab/feature.
